PICT Nestling

PICT Nestling (The Preschool and Child Enrichment Centre) is an offering from the Society for Education Training and Research (SETR). It has been set up to offer two parallel programmes, the Preschool and the Child Enrichment Centre, under its umbrella. The Preschool caters to age group of 2.4 to 5.6 years while the skill development programme is designed for children from 6 months to 11 months. Young learners in the Nestling Preschool are trained appropriately and adequately to allign themselves with the vision and mission of PICT Nestling. PICT Nestling (The Preschool and Child Enrichment Centre) structured program offers a balanced blend of Experiential discovery and the Montessori Method catering to infants from 6 months to young children aged 8 years. PICT Nestling maintains a healthy child staff ratio. Our age appropriate activities cater to the cognitive and physical needs of toddlers and young learners. They are given opportunities which aim to develop: -Aesthetic and creative expression -Discovery of the world -Language and literacy -Motor skills development -Numeracy -Personal, social and emotional development We believe that children are nourished best in natural surroundings. Engaging in natural and indigenous play helps children bloom into happy individuals. PICT Nestling has large open areas for outdoor play, exercises and games. The indoors are spacious, have child friendly washrooms, soft flooring and cushioned walls, an infirmary and has a hygienic kitchen and dining area.

Library

PICT Model School has a well equipped library to inculcate reading habits in students from the early childhood. The books open many horizons of thoughts for the little creative minds and allows them to explore a lot of new things.

Kitchen Garden

PICT Model School has adapted this very important need of today’s lives which is to grow organic vegetables and fruits in one’s own garden! We encourage our students to learn and follow the same so as to make them understand the need and importance of organic farming.

Physical Education

Exercise, Yoga, sports and other physical activities play an important role in keeping the students active and energetic. We have dedicated sessions for sports, yoga and physical education. Learners at PICT are trained to play sports like badminton, basketball and table tennis besides local and indigenous games to develop their motor skills and team spirit.
